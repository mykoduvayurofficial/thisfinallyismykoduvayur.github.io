INTRODUCTION

Koduvayur the prime Business Center for the south-west region of Palakkad district. Almost every kind of services that one needs is available in and around this place. Its hard to keep record of every one of them and use them at the time of need. MyKoduvayur brings to you the entire list and details of every kind of services that is available around this place, presented in a clean and ordered way with categories and sub-categories. One can look through it whenever needed, choose one and use it. We believe that this page will be the most useful one in this website. We hope you find it useful.
