INTRODUCTION

The lifestyle and traditions of people varies for each locality and community of people, here in Koduvayur. This Cultural Diversity results in numerous festivals to celebrate. Koduvayur Radholsavam is a Special Occasion for everyone here, regardless of their religion, gender, caste or age. From Eid, Easter and Onam to a number of other Regional Festivals spread around like the Ethanur Kummaty, Ganeshotsavam or Navarathri, there is something to celebrate each time of the year. This page throws light upon the Religious Places of Worship and the details of all the important festivals that are celebrated in and around Koduvayur.
